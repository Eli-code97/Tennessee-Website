Facts about Chattanooga

  1. Chattanooga is an urban city with a population of around 184,086.
  2. Chattanooga was founded in the year 1839 and is located in the south-eastern region of Tennessee.
  3. The average income in Chattanooga is $33,916, which is very close to the state average of 33,901.
